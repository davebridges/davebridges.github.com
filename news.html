October 2012: New Publication on the Role of PIKfyve
In collaboration with several groups at the University of Michigan, PNAS has recently published our article describing a PIKfyve hypomorphic mouse and our studies into the mechanisms of PI(3,5)P2 and PI(5)P metabolism. A link to the paper is here. Congratulations to Sergey Zolov and the corresponding author Lois Weisman.
Keystone Symposia Talk
I have accepted an invitation to give a short talk at the upcoming Keystone Symposia on Inositol Signaling in Pharmacology and Disease (a joint meeting about PI3K Signaling Pathways) at the Keystone Resort in Keystone, Colorado from 2010-02-13 to 2010-02-18. The title of my short talk will be Endolysosomal Phosphatidylinositides and the Regulation of TORC1 Localization and Function.
